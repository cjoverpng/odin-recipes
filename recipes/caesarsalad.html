Caesar Salad

Description

This caesar salad recipe is perfect for those days when you want something fresh and easy to make.

Ingredients

  • Croutons
  • 2 small garlic cloves
  • 2 tsp dijon mustard
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tsp fresh lemon juice
  • 1 1/2 tsp red wine vinegar
  • 1/3 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp sea salt, or to taste
  • 1/8 tsp black pepper
  • 1 large romaine lettuce
  • 1/3 cup parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Step 1: Making the Caesar Salad Dressing

    2. Whisk together garlic, dijon, Worcestershire, lemon juice and red wine vinegar.Slowly drizzle in extra virgin olive oil while whisking constantly.Finally, whisk in 1/2 tsp salt and 1/8 tsp black pepper, or season to taste.

  2. Step 2: Assembling the salad

    3. Rinse, dry and chop the romaine lettuce into bite-sized pieces. Place in a large serving bowl and sprinkle generously with shredded parmesan cheese and croutons. Drizzle with caesar dressing and toss gently until lettuce is evenly coated.
