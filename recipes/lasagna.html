Lasagna

Description

This lasagna recipe takes a little work, but it is so good that the effort is worth it!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound Italian sausage
  • 3/4 poung lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp white sugar
  • 4 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 1/2 tsp dried basil leaves
  • 12 lasagna noodles

Steps

  1. Step 1

    2. Gather all your ingredients.

  2. Step 2

    3. Cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic in a Dutch oven over medium heat until well browned.

  3. Step 3

    4. Stir in tomatoes and seasoning, simmer,covered, for about 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally.

  4. Step 4

    5. Boil your lasagna noodles.

  5. Step 5

    6. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F.

  6. Step 6

    7. Assemble everything in a baking dish.

  7. Step 7

    8. Bake for 25 minutes and rest for 15 minutes before serving.
